The Best Indian movies According to Sushant

Top 3 Bollywood movies of all time

3 idiots (2009)

A story of three college friends who deals with society, hard times, family and the indian way of engineering. comedy,romance,friendship,Life.

Zindagi na milegi doobara (2011)

One of the best lessons that the film brought with it was to spread the importance of taking a break in life. To think in a different way,to actually review the meaning of life.

Sardar udham (2021)

The movie is just a glimpse into the effects of British colonial rule of India, it's a biopic of a freedom fighter who waited 21 years to kill a cruel british officer who's responsible for the massacre at Jallianwala Bagh.
